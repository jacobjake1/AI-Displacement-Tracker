How to read these numbers:  Total Direct = jobs AGI/ASI eliminates outright across both waves.  Cascade Lost = additional jobs lost through spending collapse — every displaced worker stops buying, eliminating ~0.55 downstream jobs in restaurants, retail, and local services.  Total Effective = the real number of Americans who lose income.  The productivity stats show how few AGI-augmented workers are needed to produce today's full economic output — this is why rehiring never happens.
